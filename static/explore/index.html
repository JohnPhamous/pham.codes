Summer Explore Experience

Summer 2019

☀️ About the Summer Explore Experience

This opportunity is intended for individuals who tried their best in trying to get technical experience for the 2019 summer. Getting that first gig is hard and not getting one doesn't mean you're not good enough. If you tried your best but didn't win the numbers game then I want to help you out.

The Summer Explorer Experience will be awarded to an individual who is interested in the tech industry. It is a chance to learn about tech companies and different career paths you can take.

The following will be awarded to the recipient:

  • An all expense paid trip to Seattle for 2 days and 3 nights (includes travel to/from airports, flights, lodging, and food)
  • Opportunities to meet and shadow full time staff at the following companies (subject to change):
    • Microsoft
    • Google
    • Amazon
    • Stripe
    • Qualtrics
  • The itinerary for the trip will be tailored to what you want to get out of it. In your application, let me know your career goals. You can choose activities such as meeting professionals, shadowing, exploring campuses, and presenting your projects.

This experience is open to all UCR students. It is intended for individuals who have no prior professional technical experience.

📝 Requirements

To apply for this experience, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Demonstrated interest in technology (hackathons, personal projects, online courses, etc)
  • Demonstrate that you attempted to secure a summer opportunity (internship, research, etc.)
  • Should not have any prior technical experiences other than on-campus research/jobs
  • Been an active member of ACM or ACM-W for the 2018-2019 academic year as defined by the organizations
  • Submit your resume
  • Submit your LinkedIn profile
  • State which career path you're interested in from the following (can be multiple):
    • Software Engineer
    • Program Manager
    • Developer Advocate
    • Have another career path in mind? Let me know!
  • Submit a statement on why you should be picked for this experience (essay, video, website, in whatever medium you choose). A few things you can include are:
    • Why are you interested in your chosen career path?
    • What are your short-term (6—12 month) goals?
    • What are your long-term (2—5 year) goals?
  • Your selected 3 day range between August 5, 2019 to September 13, 2019
  • Feel free to send anything else

✨ Submission

Deadline to apply is July 12, 2019. You can submit your application to john@pham.codes.

If you have any questions on this opportunity or just want to chat, send me an email! I'd love to help you out.

👀 The Review Process

Your submission will be reviewed by myself and 3 other participating professionals. Below is how we will be reviewing your submissions (all personal information will be removed during the review process):

  • How much effort did you put into your submission?
  • How much effort did you put in the past year to accomplish your goals?
  • Examples of the following traits:
    • Self—engagement with technology
    • Leadership or community participation
    • Self-making

No matter the decision made on your submission, you will hear back by July 30, 2019.
